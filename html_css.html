About Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee, also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web, the HTML markup language, the URL system, and HTTP.
Born: 8 June 1955 (age 69 years), London, United Kingdom
Education: The Queen's College (1973–1976), Emanuel School (1969–1973), Sheen Mount Primary School
Awards: The President's Medal, Turing Award ·
Children: Alice Berners-Lee, Ben Berners-Lee
Parents: Mary Lee Woods, Conway Berners-Lee
Siblings: Mike Berners-Lee
Spouse: Rosemary Leith (m. 2014), Nancy Carlson (m. 1990–2011)
